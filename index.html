Regular Bullets

Standard ammunition with unlimited shots. Moderate speed and damage.

Scatter Shot

Fires 5 bullets in a wide spread pattern. Perfect for close-range combat.

Limit: 5 shots

Laser Beam

High-speed precision laser bullets. Faster but shorter range.

Limit: 5 shots

Slower missiles with massive explosion radius. Devastating area damage.

Limit: 5 shots

Explosive

Explodes on impact, damaging all tanks within blast radius.

Limit: 5 shots
🏹

Piercing

Arrow bullets that penetrate through walls and obstacles.

Limit: 5 shots
💎

Mine

Drops explosive landmines that arm after 2 seconds and explode when touched.

Limit: 5 shots
